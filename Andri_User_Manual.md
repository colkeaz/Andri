# Andri 🏪
**The Smart Inventory & POS Companion**

Welcome to Andri! This application is designed specifically for small business owners and sari-sari stores to simplify inventory management and protect profit margins using intelligent tools.

---

## 🚀 1. Getting Started

Andri is built to be simple, fast, and reliable. Because it is an **Offline-First** app, you don't need an active internet connection to process sales or add inventory. All your data stays securely on your device.

### Navigation Overview
- **Dashboard**: Your quick overview of alerts (like low stock or dead stock) and recent sales.
- **Intake (Add Stock)**: The place to add new inventory. You can use the smart camera to scan receipts or labels.
- **POS (Sell)**: Your digital cash register. Scan items or tap them on the screen to process a customer's purchase.
- **Inventory**: A complete list of all your current products and stock levels.

---

## 📸 2. Smart Intake & Adding Inventory

Adding stock doesn't have to mean typing out hundreds of items manually.

### Scanning Receipts (AI Vision)
1. Go to the **Intake** tab.
2. Tap **Scan Receipt**.
3. Align your wholesaler receipt inside the camera frame and tap the capture button.
4. The AI will automatically read the product names, quantities, and prices.
5. Review the parsed list. If any item is highlighted in red, simply tap the box to fix the missing name or zero-value price.
6. Once everything is correct, tap **Process Items** to save them to your inventory.

### Scanning Product Labels
If you just want to quickly add a single item:
1. Go to **Intake** > **Scan Label**.
2. Point your camera at the product's barcode or label.
3. The app will attempt to extract the name or barcode automatically.

### Manual Entry
You can always manually type in products by selecting **Manual Add** from the Intake tab.

## 💰 3. Profit Guard™

Inflation can sneak up on small businesses. Andri's **Profit Guard™** feature acts as your personal financial advisor.

- When you add a product via a receipt scan, the app compares the new cost price against your history.
- If the wholesale cost went up, Profit Guard will automatically suggest a new **Selling Price** (typically calculating a 15% margin) to ensure you never sell at a loss.
- You'll see these suggestions clearly marked during the Intake review process.

---

## 🛒 4. Point of Sale (POS)

Processing a customer's checkout is built for speed.

### Using the Barcode Scanner
1. Open the **Sell** tab.
2. The camera is active by default. Simply point it at an item's barcode.
3. The item will be instantly added to the current cart. A vibration (haptic feedback) confirms a successful scan.

### Quick-Add Grid
For items without barcodes (like loose bread or individual candies):
1. Scroll down to the **Quick Add** section in the **Sell** tab.
2. Tap the product tiles to add them to your cart. 
3. If an item is out of stock, the tile will be greyed out.

### Processing the Sale
1. Review the cart. Tap the **+** or **-** buttons to adjust quantities.
2. Tap the green **Finish Sale** button.
3. A confirmation screen will appear. Tap **Confirm** to complete the transaction and instantly deduct the stock from your inventory.

---

## 🔔 5. Alerts & Dead-Stock

Check your **Dashboard** regularly to keep your business healthy.

- **Low Stock**: Alerts you when an item drops below its minimum threshold (default is 5 units).
- **Dead-Stock**: Flags items that haven't sold in the last 30 days. This helps you identify products you might need to discount or stop ordering.

---

## 🛠️ 6. Troubleshooting & Tips

- **Camera Not Loading?** Ensure you've granted camera permissions in your phone's settings.
- **Receipt Scanner Missed Something?** Ensure you have good lighting and the receipt is flat. If the AI misses a decimal, simply tap the price box and type it in manually (e.g., `7.50`).
- **Data Security**: Your sales data is safely stored on your device using professional-grade database transactions. Even if the app closes unexpectedly mid-sale, your stock counts remain accurate.
